My Favorite Games to Play

Call of Duty
I play many different games, some simulation, and some arcade and story-based games. However, most of my time is on Call of Duty. I usually either play Warzone or Vanguard with friends. Although sometimes the game makes me want to pull my hair out, I still enjoy the thrill of it. I am not the best player in the world, nor do I claim to be, but it sure is fun!
NBA 2K
NBA 2K was one of the first games that I ever personally bought and played. As I was formerly a basketball player, it makes sense. I've always loved playing against other people in "my sport" virtually, because I feel like I have at least a small advantage. During this year's release, I mainly play in the Rec with my Cousin and some of his friends. For the upcoming release, I plan to join a Pro-AM team and try to make some money playing.
Grand Theft Auto 5
I didn't really want to be specific on the titles, but considering that this game has been out for nine years, and there's still no new release, I feel obligated to. GTA 5 is one of the weirdest, challenging, yet fun games that I've ever played. I recently started getting back into it, and I'm currently having quite a bit of fun!
