Who is SKIBZ?

How did you get into video games?
I started playing video games around the age of five with my older cousins. They used to always demolish me, but I still had fun playing. Eventually I got a GameCube, yes, you read that right, and have been playing ever since. I have since upgraded, and now play on Playstation!
Who do you play with?
I usually play with my friends from either high school or here at college. I also have multiple family members that I play with, so it is always a good time in the chat.
Why do you like to play video games?
I like to play video games because it gives you a chance to interact and do things with people that may not live close to you, which is a huge bonus while in college. Rather than talking on the phone, you can talk while playing video games, which is way more fun anyways! I also just love the competitiveness that comes along with playing as well.
